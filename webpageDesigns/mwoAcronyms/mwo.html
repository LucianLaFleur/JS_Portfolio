(ECM) : Electonic Counter-Measures

Colloquially known as "the magic Jesus box", this hides from radar detection. The Electronic Counter-Measure system slows enemy target acquisition, making it harder to get a lock. Laser tagging and relying on your good ol' eye-holes helps to counteract this. Even a mech running ECM will show up on heat-vision/night vision. In theory, a cloaking device would be a "VCM", visual counter-measure.

(LRM) : Long-Range Missiles

Long-Range missiles use targeting, often with a tagging system and have the advantage of a high arc, going above cover and other terrain obstacles. While called "long-range" they can be fired from as short as 200 meters effectively, reducing flight time and increasing accuracy. Note that LRMs deal non-pinpoint damage, spread across a mech. Thus, they are "support" rather than direct-attacking brawlers.

(AMS) : Anti-missile System

The anti-missile system is either ballistic or laser while lasers don't suffer from ammo capacity limits they're liable to make your mech overheat. A skilled pilot might even force the lightshow and hammer lots of missiles to force an overheat if the opposing pilot doesn't shut off their AMS.
